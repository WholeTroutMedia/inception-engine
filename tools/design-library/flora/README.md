# Flora AI (Luma Labs Fauna)

Flora AI (represented by Luma's Fauna beta) focuses on giving users structural control over generative AI rather than just a text prompt.

## Account Status

- **Status:** Logged In

## Internal Editing Suite: Fauna Canvas

Fauna is an "Intelligent Canvas" currently in beta.

- **Ideation:** Create comprehensive mood boards directly from text descriptions.
- **Stylization:** Turn photos into stylized illustrations perfectly matched to the mood board.
- **Director Control:** Make video from static images with explicit camera motion pathing.

## Usage in Creative Liberation Engine

Used during IDEATE phases to generate visual mood boards and creative directions before transitioning to the Media Pipeline for final video rendering. Matches the "Creative Direction" workflow.
